COVID Cases and Vaccinations

With the Coronavirus drastically changing our world over the last year, we wanted to take a look at how The United States has been doing in terms of cases. Now that vaccine distributions are on the rise as well, our goal was to figure out if there were any correlations between state cases and vaccine distributions.

Our site includes a scatter plot of vaccine distributions and allocations by sttae, a lollipop chart of COVID cases by state, a heatmap of cases for the country, and a Texas choropleth for vaccine distributions by Texas counties.
